# GIS Solutions

SaltGIS provides comprehensive geospatial solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of modern organizations. From data collection to advanced analytics, our platform scales with your requirements.

## Data Collection & Management

### Field Data Collection
- **Offline Capabilities**: Collect data even without internet connectivity
- **Real-time Sync**: Automatic synchronization when connection is restored
- **Custom Forms**: Build forms tailored to your specific data needs
- **GPS Integration**: Accurate location tracking with built-in GPS support

### Data Storage & Organization
- **Cloud Storage**: Secure, scalable cloud-based storage
- **Version Control**: Track changes and maintain data integrity
- **Access Control**: Role-based permissions and security
- **API Access**: Programmatic access to your data

## Spatial Analysis

### Advanced Analytics
- **Buffer Analysis**: Create and analyze spatial buffers
- **Overlay Operations**: Combine multiple spatial datasets
- **Network Analysis**: Route optimization and network modeling
- **Statistical Analysis**: Spatial statistics and pattern recognition

### Modeling & Prediction
- **Spatial Interpolation**: Predict values across geographic areas
- **Hotspot Analysis**: Identify clusters and patterns
- **Risk Assessment**: Model and predict spatial risks
- **Scenario Planning**: Test different scenarios and outcomes

### Dashboards & Reports
