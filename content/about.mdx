# About SaltGIS

SaltGIS is a leading provider of advanced geospatial software and location intelligence solutions. Founded with a vision to democratize access to powerful GIS tools, we've been helping organizations make better location-based decisions since our inception.

## Our Mission

We believe that location data should be accessible, understandable, and actionable for everyone. Our mission is to build software that transforms complex geospatial information into clear insights that drive better business outcomes.

## What We Do

- **Data Collection**: Streamlined field data collection with offline capabilities
- **Spatial Analysis**: Advanced analytical tools for complex geospatial modeling
- **Visualization**: Beautiful, interactive maps and dashboards
- **Integration**: Seamless connectivity with existing GIS workflows

## Our Values

- **Innovation**: Constantly pushing the boundaries of what's possible in GIS
- **Accessibility**: Making complex geospatial tools easy to use
- **Reliability**: Enterprise-grade software you can depend on
- **Community**: Supporting the open geospatial ecosystem
